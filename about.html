The Mei Ling Philosophy

Founded by culinary visionary Zhang Wei and hospitality expert Noor Al Mahmood, Mei Ling was born out of a desire to bring authentic, uncompromising Chinese fine dining to Manama. The restaurant takes its name from the famous 20th‑century opera singer, reflecting the artistry and drama we pour into every dish.

Our kitchen is led by Chef Li Huang, formerly of three‑Michelin‑starred kitchens in Hong Kong. Every ingredient is hand‑selected, from the live seafood tanks to the hand‑pickled Sichuan peppers. At Mei Ling, dining is theatre — and you are the honoured guest.
