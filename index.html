Our Heritage

The Art of Chinese Gastronomy

Mei Ling is named after the legendary Chinese opera singer, embodying grace and passion. Our chefs, trained in Beijing and Hong Kong, use time‑honoured techniques with the finest ingredients sourced globally.

From hand‑pulled noodles to delicately steamed dim sum, every plate is a masterpiece of balance — sweet, sour, salty, bitter, and umami in perfect harmony.
